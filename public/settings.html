📧 Email Notifications

Email alerts are sent via SMTP (nodemailer). Configure the variables below in your .env file and restart the server. Once set, use the test button to verify delivery.

EMAIL_HOSTSMTP host (e.g. smtp.gmail.com)
EMAIL_PORT587 for TLS, 465 for SSL
EMAIL_USERYour email address
EMAIL_PASSApp password (not your login password)
EMAIL_FROM"MahattaART Tasks <you@example.com>"
